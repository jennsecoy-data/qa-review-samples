# QA Review Checklist

This checklist is used to review documents, records, forms, content, and task outputs for accuracy, completeness, consistency, formatting, and guideline compliance.

## 1. Source-to-Final Comparison

- Does the final version match the approved source material?
- Were all requested edits applied correctly?
- Are there any differences between the source and final version that were not requested?
- Do text, images, labels, captions, instructions, and formatting align with the approved version?
- Are paper, online, alternate-format, and supporting materials consistent when applicable?

## 2. Accuracy Review

- Are names, titles, dates, numbers, labels, and references correct?
- Are item numbers, form names, document titles, or record details accurate?
- Are calculations, totals, or values correct if present?
- Is the information supported by the provided source or instruction?
- Are there any spelling, grammar, punctuation, or wording errors that could affect meaning?

## 3. Completeness Review

- Are all required sections included?
- Are any fields, notes, images, captions, labels, pages, or instructions missing?
- Are all required edits, comments, or reviewer notes addressed?
- Is the final version complete enough to move forward?
- Are all supporting materials present and aligned with the main document?

## 4. Consistency Review

- Is formatting consistent across the full document or content set?
- Are fonts, spacing, capitalization, punctuation, and headings consistent?
- Are repeated terms, titles, labels, and instructions written the same way throughout?
- Are tables, bullet lists, captions, and numbered items formatted consistently?
- Do multiple versions of the same content match where they should?

## 5. Content Quality Review

- Is the language clear, professional, and easy to understand?
- Does the content follow the required tone, format, and instructions?
- Are there any unclear, awkward, duplicated, or incomplete statements?
- Are visual elements placed correctly and easy to interpret?
- Does the final content meet the intended purpose?

## 6. Accessibility and Alternate Format Review

- Are text-to-speech notes, accommodations, or alternate-format instructions included if required?
- Are image captions, labels, or descriptions accurate and consistent?
- Are formatting choices clear and readable?
- Are required accessibility-related notes applied across all relevant versions?
- Are online and paper versions aligned where needed?

## 7. Issue Documentation

When an issue is found, document:

- What the issue is
- Where the issue appears
- What source, requirement, or instruction it conflicts with
- Whether the issue is minor, moderate, or major
- Whether the issue appears once or repeatedly
- Recommended correction or next action
- Final review status

## 8. Review Status Options

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pass | No issues found and content is ready to move forward |
| Needs Review | Minor issue or uncertainty requires follow-up |
| Needs Correction | Issue must be corrected before approval |
| Blocked | Review cannot continue due to missing information or access |
| Complete | Corrections were verified and final review is finished |

## 9. Example QA Notes

| Review Area | Issue Found | Recommended Action | Status |
|---|---|---|---|
| Accuracy | Date in final version does not match approved source | Correct date before approval | Needs Correction |
| Completeness | Required accessibility note is missing | Add note to online version | Needs Correction |
| Consistency | Caption punctuation differs across two forms | Standardize caption formatting | Needs Review |
| Formatting | Heading spacing differs from rest of document | Adjust formatting for consistency | Needs Review |
| Source Match | Final text matches approved wording | No action needed | Pass |

## 10. Final Review Questions

Before marking a review complete, confirm:

- Were all source materials checked?
- Were all required edits verified?
- Were issues documented clearly?
- Were repeated patterns or workflow concerns noted?
- Were corrections reviewed after updates?
- Is the final version accurate, complete, consistent, and ready for use?
